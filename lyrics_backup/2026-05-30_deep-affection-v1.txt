[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Midnight shadows falling on the hardwood floor
Your silhouette is all I'm looking for
Drawing closer to the fire in your eyes
Losing track of every single compromise

[Verse 1]
The quiet rhythm of the rain upon the tin
I open up the door and let the midnight in
You're leaning heavy on the shoulder of my coat
I catch the steady little tremble in your throat

[Pre-Chorus]
The world is fading to a flicker in the dark
A slow surrender adding fuel unto the spark
My hands are tracing all the secrets you conceal
I want to show you everything I truly feel

[Chorus]
This deep affection pulling like a heavy tide
A restless current we no longer have to hide
I'm falling under, swept away by what I see
A sweet devotion crashing down on you and me

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, wrapped up in the warmth of you
Yeah, nothing else I'd rather do

[Verse 2]
The amber glow across the room begins to fade
We lose ourselves inside the quiet that we made
You pull me closer with a whisper in the air
And leave me tangled in the wildness of your hair
